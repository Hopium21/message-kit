# Examples

Discover some of the most popular Apps built with MessageKit.

> Message `messagekit` in Converse to get started.

## Bots

These are some open source examples of mini-apps built with MessageKit.

#### 💧 Faucetbot

- **Description**: Delivers faucet funds to devs on testnet.
- **How to use**: [Send a DM](https://converse.xyz/dm/faucetbot.eth) to `faucetbot.eth` in Converse.
- **Source Code**: [Bot](https://github.com/fabriguespe/faucet-bot) | [Frame](https://github.com/fabriguespe/tx-receipt-frame)

#### 🛒 The general store

- **Description**: E-commerce app for ordering goodies in hackathons
- **How to use**: [Send a DM](https://converse.xyz/dm/thegeneralstore.eth) to`thegeneralstore.eth` in Converse.
- **Source Code**: [Bot](https://github.com/fabriguespe/thegeneralstore-bot)

#### 📅 Daily wordle

- **Description**: Play daily to the WORDLE game by du8 through messaging
- **How to use**: [Send a DM](https://converse.xyz/dm/dailywordle.eth) to `dailywordle.eth` in Converse.

### 🎫 Invite Bot

- **Description**: Invite bot for Events linked to a Notion db.
- **How to use**: [Send a DM](https://converse.xyz/dm/invitebot.eth) to `invitebot.eth` in Converse.
- **Source Code**: [Bot](https://github.com/fabriguespe/invite-bot)

#### 🪨 Mintframe

- **Description**: Turn a Zora url into a mint frame
- **How to use**: [Send a DM](https://converse.xyz/dm/0xB7d51dD8331551D2FA0d185F8Ba08DcCd71499aD) to `mintframe.eth` in Converse.
- **Source Code**: [Bot](https://github.com/fabriguespe/coinbase-mint-frame-bot) | [Frame](https://github.com/builders-garden/xmtp-coinbase-mint-frame)

## Frames

All these Frames are compatible with the [Open Frames](https://www.openframes.xyz/) standard.

- 🧠 [Brain Frame](https://frame.brianknows.org/): AI multipurpose frame.
- 💸 [Tx Receipt](https://github.com/fabriguespe/tx-receipt-frame) : Frame that acts a transaction receipt.
- 💸 [BaseFrame](./directory/baseframe.mdx): A dev-oriented frame designed for creating transactions on Base.
- 📊 [Polls](https://fc-polls-five.vercel.app/): Create polls on messaging apps.
- 🌿 [Mint Frame](https://github.com/fabriguespe/mint-frame/): Frame for redirecting users to mint on Zora
- 🖼️ [Wordle](https://openframedl.vercel.app/): Wordle in a frame - daily or random game.
- 🌍 [Farguessr](https://farguessr.vercel.app/): Guess the distance between two countries
- 🪨 [Rock Paper Scissors](https://xmtp-frame-rock-paper-scissors.vercel.app/): Rock paper scissors game.
- 🎰 [Slot machine](https://slot-machine-frame.vercel.app/): Slot machine open frame.
